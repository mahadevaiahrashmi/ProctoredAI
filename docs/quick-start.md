# Quick Start — Taking Your Exam

The one-page version. For full detail, tips, and troubleshooting, see the
[User Manual](user_manual.md).

> **What this is:** you pick a topic, the app creates a short exam, watches your webcam while
> you take it, grades you instantly, and gives you an AI tutor that talks you through the results.

---

## You need

- A computer with a **webcam** and **microphone**
- A modern browser — **Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Firefox**
- An **internet** connection
- To be **alone in a quiet, well-lit room**, with your **phone out of sight**

---

## 5 steps

| # | Do this | You'll see |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **1** | Type **your name** and an **exam topic** (e.g. "World History"), then click **Generate Exam & Proceed**. | A few seconds while your exam is built. |
| **2** | Read the welcome message, then click **Start System Check**. | The permission screen. |
| **3** | Click **Allow** when the browser asks for **camera** and **microphone**. When both show a **green check**, click **Start Exam**. | Your live camera and the first question. |
| **4** | Answer the **5 questions**. Click an option (multiple-choice) or type your answer (written). Use **Previous / Next** to move — answers are kept. Watch the **timer** (top-right). | A green **"System Secure"** status, or a red alert if something is flagged. |
| **5** | On the last question click **Submit Exam**, then **Submit** to confirm. | Your **score**, a **summary**, per-question **feedback**, and the **AI Tutor**. |

**Ask the tutor anything about your exam** — type a question and press **Send**. It answers in
writing *and reads the answer aloud*. Click **Return to Home** when you're done.

---

## Stay on the right side of the proctor

✔ Keep your **face centered** and in view  ✔ Stay **alone**  ✔ Keep your **phone away**  ✔ Don't open other apps

A flag is a *potential* concern the system noted — not an automatic penalty.

---

## Two things to know

- **The timer auto-submits.** If it reaches zero, your exam is sent with whatever you've entered (about 1½ minutes per question).
- **Don't refresh the page.** Your exam lives in this browser tab only — refreshing or opening a new tab can lose your progress.

---

## If something goes wrong

| Problem | Fix |
| --- | --- |
| Camera/mic shows a **red X** | Click the **padlock** in the address bar, set Camera + Microphone to **Allow**, then **refresh**. |
| **"Failed to generate exam"** | Try again, or use a slightly different topic. |
| You see a **Quantum Physics** sample exam | Your session was lost — go back to the **home page** and start at step 1. |
| The **tutor didn't speak** | Check your tab isn't muted; the written answer always appears anyway. |
